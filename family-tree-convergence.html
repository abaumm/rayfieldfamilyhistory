How the Lines Connect

A guide to the six families in The Rayfields — A Family History

The Rayfield surname runs straight down the middle — the spine of the book, from a colonial farmer in 1670 to Vernon Jack Rayfield. Five other families married in, each with its own chapter. All of it meets at the bottom, where Vernon married Opal Robinson and the two halves of the book become one. Cards with a link open that person’s profile.
